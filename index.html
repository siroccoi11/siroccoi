Welcome to SIRROCOI (U) LIMITED

SIROCCOI is a reputable company that provides challenged engineering services such as construction, installations, refurbishment in manufacturing, Hydro, Mechanical, Chemical plants and oil reticulation, refineries just to mention but a few. Our goal is to exceed our client’s demands by offering outstanding quality work with increased flexibility, and greater value thus optimizing investments. We are distinguished by functional and technical expertise combined by hands-on experience thereby ensuring that our clients receive quality and professional services. The state-of-art quality assurance equipment enables us easily achieve our clients’ expectations. .

  • VISION

    • Increase reputation in the domestic market; • Grow a permanent and sustainable manner with maximum profitability; • Raise the confidence levels of our customers; • Increase the productive and administrative efficiency of the company; • Optimize the company's Safety, Health and Environment policy.

  • MISSION

    Perform public and private construction works in order to meet the requirements of the market, with the principles of quality, trust, credibility and respect for the environment, thus serving our customers with the most professionalism.

  • VALUES

    SIROCCOI (U) LTD believes that, upholding the Companies reputation is paramount. Our reputation will be upheld if we act with honesty and integrity in all our dealings and we do what we think is right at all times within the legitimate role of business. The Companies also firmly believe in the fundamental importance of the promotion of trust, openness, teamwork and professionalism.
